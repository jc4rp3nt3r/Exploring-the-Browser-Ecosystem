Falsey Values

Truthy Values

assert(true, 'true is (of course) Truthy'); assert('String', 'Strings are Truthy'); assert(1, 'All non-zero numbers are Truthy') assert({}, 'Objects are Truthy') assert([], 'Arrays are Truthy') assert('up, up, down, down, left, right, left, right, B, A', 'EVERYTHING not Falsey is Truthy')
